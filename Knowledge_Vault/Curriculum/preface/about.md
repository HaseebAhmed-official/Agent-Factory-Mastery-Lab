| Three Delivery Modes | Traditional reading, TutorClaw (AI tutor), FTE Development Plugin (AI building partner) |
| 10-80-10 Pattern | Intent-setting, execution, verification framework for education and operations |
| Agentic Enterprise | Organizations building hybrid workforces combining human judgment with machine intelligence |
