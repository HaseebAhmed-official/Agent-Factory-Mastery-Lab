## How to Open

1. Open `INDEX.html` in any browser
2. Click any lesson card to open its presentation
3. Navigate slides with arrow keys or clicking

Or open individual lesson HTMLs directly.

---

## Regenerating

- **All presentations**: Re-run `Finish` for each lesson
- **INDEX only**: `python3 scripts/generate-index.py`
- **Single lesson**: `python3 scripts/generate-html.py --content path/to/content.json`
